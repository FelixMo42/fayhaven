To Rock's dismay, in the distance, he spots tents. Moments before he had been certain of his impending doom. It would have been a slow and painful death, but at least he would have died doing what he loved. Few people got that luckily in life.

It had been nearly a week since he seen gotten lost off trail. No one knew he was out here, and no one would notice he had gone missing for quite a while. His life was a solitary and lonely one.

But now, he was saved. Consciously, he approached his would-be rescuers.

There were 4 of them huddled around a small propane stove.
